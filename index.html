Privacy Policy

Plain English. Last updated June 2026.

What we collect
  • Account info — username and password (password is hashed by Supabase and never readable by anyone, including us).
  • Profile info — display name, and optionally: Venmo/Cash App/Zelle handles, phone number, email address, and a profile photo. All optional, only used to help your group settle up.
  • Expense data — descriptions, amounts, dates, categories, notes, who paid, who was involved, and the city. Visible only to members of your group.
  • Receipt photos — if you scan a receipt, the photo is stored so your group can view it later. See "Receipt scanning" below.
  • Payment records — when someone marks a settlement as paid, we log who paid who and how much.
  • Device preferences — dark mode preference and whether you've seen the onboarding tips. Stored only on your device (localStorage), never on our servers.
Receipt scanning

When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to Google Gemini (Google's AI service) to extract the totals and line items. Google receives the image to process it. We do not store the image with Google — their API processes it and returns the text. Google's privacy policy governs how they handle API data: policies.google.com/privacy.

If you'd prefer not to use AI scanning, you can always enter amounts manually.

Where your data lives

All data (accounts, expenses, photos) is stored on Supabase, which runs on AWS infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Supabase's privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy.

Who can see your data
  • Your group members can see all expenses, payment records, and profile info (including any payment handles you've added) within your shared group.
  • We (the app developer) can access data as the Supabase project owner, but we don't look at your data unless you ask us to help with a problem.
  • No one else. We don't sell data, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything other than running this app.
Your rights
  • Leave a group — you can leave any group at any time from the Crew tab. Your past expenses remain in the group log (since they affect other members' balances) but you are removed as a participant going forward.
  • Delete your data — to request full deletion of your account and data, open a GitHub issue or email the developer. We'll remove your account within 30 days.
  • Export — use the "Export CSV" button in the History tab to download all expense data at any time.
What we don't do
  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • No selling or sharing your data with third parties (beyond the infrastructure providers above)
  • No profiling or analytics on your spending behaviour
Contact

Questions or deletion requests: open an issue at github.com/benfeldman93 or email benfeldman933@gmail.com.
